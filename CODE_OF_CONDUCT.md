# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical issues and observable behavior.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Assume good intent while asking clear questions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Disruptive behavior that blocks healthy collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may remove comments, commits, issues, or contributors that violate this policy.

## Reporting

Please report incidents through the contact path in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md), with subject `Code of Conduct`.
